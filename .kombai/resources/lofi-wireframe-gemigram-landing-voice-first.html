📋 Design Notes — Voice-First Redesign

  • Key Change: The Voice Orb replaces the text-heavy hero. It's the first thing users see — tap and speak.
  • BotFather Inspiration: Like Telegram's BotFather, the creation flow is conversational. User describes → system materializes.
  • Reduced Text: Marketing copy is cut by ~60%. Focus on action, not explanation.
  • Trust Bar Removed: Fake brand logos ("ORACLE.NODE", etc.) replaced with real stats bar.
  • About Us Link: Added in footer and final CTA section for the new /about page.
  • Components to Reuse: EnterpriseHeader, BentoFeatures, AuthOverlay, EnterpriseFooter
  • New Components: VoiceOrb, HowItWorks, StatsBar, FinalCTA
  • Theme: All cards use unified glass-subtle/glass-medium tiers. No more sovereign-glass vs glass-strong confusion.
